How Nexus Services Works

Quick Summary

Select your desired service, enter your email address, and complete the UPI payment. After payment verification, you will receive an email containing a service-specific form. You must provide accurate details (such as username or post link) based on the service selected.

Detailed Process

1. Browse and select the service you want.
2. Add the service to your cart.
3. Enter a valid email address.
4. Complete payment using UPI.

Once your payment is verified, you will receive an email containing a form. This form must be filled correctly according to the service purchased:

• For Likes → Provide the post or reel link.
• For Followers/Subscribers → Provide your username.
• For Watch Time or Views → Provide the correct video link.

⚠ If incorrect or invalid details are submitted, the service will not be processed. Please ensure all information is accurate before submission.
